About My Class

My class that I am including in this webpage is Music 202. Music 202 is Rock N Roll History II from 1975-Present. This class goes over the history of rock music from the year 1975 to the present day. So far we have covered mainstream rock in the seventies, punk rock, and new wave music.

Fun Facts about Rock History II

Some fun facts about this class are that it takes place entirely online due to social distancing concerns and university protocol. This class also has its textbook provided by TopHat instead of by a physical medium or other online service. Most of the songs that we study in this class are available on the Spotify streaming service. And finally, a shocking and perhaps the most important fact is that you don't need any web development experience to take this class!
